**Ecosystem mapping and partnership development in regenerative agriculture.**

I work at the intersection of regenerative agriculture, structured landscape research, and partnership development. I build intelligence assets that help organizations see who is operating in the ecosystem, where activity clusters, and how relationships could form around shared goals.

**[Case studies](/case-studies/)**, including *[Mapping the Regenerative Agriculture Ecosystem: From Landscape to Network](/case-study/)* on the U.S. regenerative agriculture landscape and moving from roster to network.
